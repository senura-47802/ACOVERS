About Ananda College Olympiad Society

The Ananda College Olympiad Society is a distinguished academic society dedicated to promoting intellectual excellence, critical thinking, and a strong competitive spirit among the students of Ananda College, Colombo. The society aims to identify and nurture talented students with a passion for advanced learning, guiding them towards success in national and international Olympiad competitions across a wide range of disciplines, including mathematics and science.Through well-structured training programs, workshops, seminars, and practice examinations, the society encourages members to explore concepts beyond the school curriculum and develop strong analytical and problem-solving skills. It also fosters teamwork, discipline, and perseverance, creating an environment where students can learn collaboratively and challenge their intellectual limits.
